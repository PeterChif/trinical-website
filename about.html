Get to Know Us

The company was established by Shakinah and John Doe in their school library. They began this project as one of the core founders was struggling with finding the right skin products for their skin condition. So, one day in the school Library Shakinah said to John that since she has been struggling to find any reliable products why not create their own. Trinical Skincare is a skincare friend, that is meant to help anyone with whatever skin condition, to have better healthier options for their skin products.
